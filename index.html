Gameplay

Metarrior is an insanely simple to play yet hard to master match-3 puzzle game, combining RPG elements with empire wars, majestic dragons, puzzle quests and PvP ...

Players make use of their resources to summon the most powerful heroes, supporting them on the way to fight the cruelty and conquer the dark forces.

heroes

Form a team of 1 - 5 heroes to engage in the battle. Each hero has several skill slots, in which each skill will be triggered in some timing during the combat based on the number of matching puzzles

There is a list of 53 heroes divided into 6 types: Elf, Dwarf, Halfblood, Warrior, Guardian and Dark

PET ISLANDS

Lead your mighty troops to victory by summoning legendary dragons to help your Heroes in the battle (Feature coming soon!)

Summon card

Summon epic heroes and lead your mighty troops of maximum 5 heroes to gain victory of PvP arena duels!

Equipments

Prepare for your battle by equipping your Heroes with the best items such as armor, boots, belt,etc... and powerful skills. Upgrading equipment will strengthen your fighting ability.

land

Build a mighty castle in the sky, where you can farm resources for your empires! (Feature coming soon!)
